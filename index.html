What is HIIT?

HIIT stands for High-Intensity Interval Training. This type of workout comprises of short intervals of high intensity exercise that elicit 85%-95% max heart rate with recovery periods in between.

Note: There are many different paradigms of HIIT, and this program will generate a HIIT workout that follows the "4 x 4" regimen which requires four intervals of high-intensity exercise that ellicits 85-95% max heart rate with periods of recovery in between.

What are the benefits of HIIT?

  • Helps to burn a lot of calories in a short amount of time
  • Increases the metabolic rate even after the workout
  • Helps to burn fat
  • May result in muscle gain
  • Improves oxygen consumption
  • Reduces heart rate and blood pressure
